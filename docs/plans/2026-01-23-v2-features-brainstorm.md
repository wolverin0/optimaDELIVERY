# v2.0 Premium Features Brainstorm

**Date:** 2026-01-23
**Status:** Paused - Prioritizing security/code fixes

## Goals Identified
1. **Increase revenue per tenant** - Higher tier pricing
2. **Expand market** - Attract new customer segments (larger restaurants, chains)
3. **Competitive differentiation** - Unique features competitors don't have

## Strategic Approaches Explored

### A) Analytics-First Premium (RECOMMENDED)
Focus on data insights that small restaurants can't get elsewhere.
- Revenue reports, peak hours analysis
- Popular items tracking
- Customer ordering patterns
- Low implementation cost (data already exists)
- Clear value prop: "See what's selling, when, and to whom"

### B) Marketing & Engagement Suite
- Promotions and discount codes
- Email capture
- WhatsApp notifications
- Loyalty points system
- More competitive space but high perceived value

### C) Operations & Efficiency Pack
- Multi-location support
- Inventory alerts
- Staff scheduling integration
- Kitchen analytics
- Targets growing restaurants/chains
- Higher implementation cost, higher LTV

## Recommended Strategy
Start with **A (Analytics)** for Basic tier, layer in **B (Marketing)** for Pro tier.
